Multi-Root Analyzer

Version: 0.1.0, Authors: Nathan Miller, Logan Johnson, Kris Kvilekval and Dmitry Fedorov

This algorithm takes user clicks as inputs and tracks the apex of an organ and reports out the tip angle of the apex. Initially the tip is located with a user click and is subsequently tracked using a corner detector in combination with a nearest neighbor tracking approach.
